BTS

Bangtan Sonyeondan

BTS has been taking the world by strom for a while now,and there are no signs of them slowing down. With a massive following on a global scale, if you haven't heard of this prominent K-Pop boy band by now, you should start getting used to seeing them everywhere!

Hailing from South Korea, BTS is now the biggest boy band in the world, topping charts and setting records. Since their debut in 2013, the seven member group - which consists of RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V and Jung Kook - have grown beyond the bounds of traditional K-pop acts, making a splash everywhere from Chile to California. Their devoted followers, styled as "ARMY," have kept them trending on social media at most public appearances, while their musical releases, like the latest one, "Boy With Luv" have broken records on YouTube and international and domestic charts.

BTS

The name BTS was originally an acronym for "Bangtan Sonyeondan," which translates roughly to "Bulletproof Boy Scouts" in English from the original Korean word which is a reference to resilience. But BTS decided to rebrand their name in 2017 to now mean "Beyond the Scene."

ARMY & BTS

BTS fans call themselves ARMY which is an acronym for Adorable Representative MC for Youth. You don't have to be a K-Pop fan to know that the ARMY is one dedicated fan base. We love and support BTS unconditionally and the same love is reciprocated by them through everything they do. They have always been humble throughout their journey from having nothing when they started to now, achieving what they deserve and surely in future too. Their journey is amazing and very inspiring.Thinking about the magnitude of their worldwide success is a tear-jerker in itself.

Now, Let me tell you a few reasons why I love them.
  • THEIR MUSIC

    Some of their songs are full of hype, while some have extremely beautiful depth to them. The list of awards speaks for itself, doesn't it? Personally, their music has helped me cope through sad situations also. They helped many people in coping up through the toughest and roughest patches of life.


  • THEIR CHOROEGRAPHY

    Their choreography for EVERY music video is on point. They sweat themselves out practing the choreography and always try to show their best to their fans. Ofcourse their hardword pays. Their choreography is very graceful and always matches with the feeling and meaning of the song making the viewers feel the song even when they dont understand the language.


  • MEANING TO THEIR SONGS

    The lyrics of their songs have a deep influence. When I started to read the lyrics, I found the situations in the lyrics very realtable. They spread the message of Loving Yourself and knowing yourself. Their themes are majorly on how to overcome the critisism that we face, self love, and other heart touching topics.


  • LOVE TOWARDS ARMY

    They LOVE ARMY and they never miss a chance to express how much we mean to them. THey always stay connected with us via social media. The fact that they honestly share their happiness and sadness with us makes us feel very special. They always send messages via twitter, weverse, fan cafes, do live vedios to express themselves, and also respond very well to everyone. The bond between BTS and ARMY is always special.


  • FUN, HAPPY AND CRAZY PERSONALITIES

    Forever making us laugh with their memes and their meme-faces, playing games, making cute aegyo faces.


  • THEIR HARDWORK , LOVE FOR EACH OTHER

    They work very hard for all their songs, lyrics, choregraphies, concerts etc. They indeed paved their way to where they are now. They also love and respect each other. They have become soo close to each other that they are indeed like a family.
